Engineering Services:

Drafting – Our drafting services include mechanical design for 2D and 3D projects. We use a combination of SolidWorks and AutoCAD depending on your needs.

We will create models based on your design. Each model will be created with individual parts that are ready for fabrication.

If requested, we will develop engineering sheets. All our drafting sheets come with a full BOM, dimensioning, revision history, and title block. Assembly and welding documents can be created as well if requested.

Documentation – We specialize in Hazard Analysis ISO 14971 and Risk Assessment ISO 9001 for your mechanical project. We provide full collaboration and documentation for your project as you progress through each phase.

Hazard analysis can be produced from a top-down approach or bottom-up depending on your project requirements. Failure modes and effects analysis (FMEA) can be developed as well depending on your project’s needs.

Calculations – We can provide you with calculations for your project to verify it is meeting your needs. Our services include kinematic, dynamic, simple structural loading, HVAC, and fluids calculations.
